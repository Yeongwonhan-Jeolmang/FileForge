# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming community, we pledge to make participation in FileForge a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Abusive or derogatory comments
- Publishing private information without consent
- Other conduct that harms project participants

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the project maintainers.
